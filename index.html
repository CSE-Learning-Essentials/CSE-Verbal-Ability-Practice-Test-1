  • A) A sudden setback or failure
  • B) The end of the planning stage
  • C) A long period of disappointment
  • D) An ongoing process of work

  • A) Open and public
  • B) Hidden and secret
  • C) Legal and authorized
  • D) Chaotic and disorganized
  • E) Well-known and obvious

4. The beauty of the sunset over the ocean was so breathtaking that it left her with an INEFFABLE sense of peace, beyond words.

  • A) Easily explained or described
  • B) Impossible to express in words
  • C) Ordinary and unremarkable
  • D) Quickly forgotten
  • E) Pleasant but harsh in nature

5. His plan for the new business was still INCHOATE, lacking clear structure and direction.

  • A) Unfinished or incomplete
  • B) Easily understood and clear
  • C) Ready for immediate action
  • D) Clearly defined and precise
  • E) Fully developed and organized

  • A) Lengthy path
  • B) Narrow and steep
  • C) Vibrant atmosphere
  • D) Full of danger or risk
  • E) Bumpy with uncomfortable experience

  • A) Completely neutral in scent
  • B) Absent of any odor or fragrance
  • C) Lacking any noticeable fragrance
  • D) Overpowering and unpleasantly strong

  • A) Afraid and withdrawn
  • B) Eager to cooperate and comply
  • C) Politely respectful of authority
  • D) Indifferent and uninterested

  • A) Demonstrating a lack of interest or concern
  • B) Displaying open criticism and sarcasm
  • C) Exhibiting a feeling of superiority and disregard
  • D) Conveying sincere understanding and sympathy

10. His OBSEQUIOUS behavior towards the CEO, constantly agreeing with everything she said, made his colleagues uncomfortable.

  • A) Demonstrating genuine admiration and respect
  • B) Showing an unwillingness to cooperate or be helpful
  • C) Acting in an excessively deferential or ingratiating manner
  • D) Displaying confidence and independence in interactions
